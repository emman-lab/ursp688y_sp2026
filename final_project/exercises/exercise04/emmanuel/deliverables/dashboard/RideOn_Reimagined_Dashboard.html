Ride On Reimagined Equity Audit

This dashboard presents a scheduled-service equity analysis of Ride On Reimagined Phase 1. It compares the January 2025 GTFS schedule with the June 2025 Phase 1 schedule and tests whether higher-transit-need tracts received larger scheduled service gains.

Central Question

Did the redesign produce larger scheduled service gains in higher-transit-need Census tracts than in lower-transit-need tracts?

Transit Need Index

The index combines ACS tract measures for households without vehicles, poverty, older adults, disability, and related indicators. GTFS stops and trips are then summarized within a 0.25-mile buffer of each tract.

Scope

The analysis measures scheduled service supply. It does not measure reliability, crowding, safety, affordability, lived rider experience, or access to destinations.

Finding Summary

The Phase 1 schedule did not show a clear equity-prioritized service increase. On average, high-need tracts lost scheduled weekday trips while moderate-low-need tracts gained service.
